Interactive

1) iSpy

iSpy is a creative interactive game created for children. The website originates for Television New Zealand’s Heihei children’s gaming app. You can explore five places all located to the beautiful country of New Zealand. All well-known geothermal attractions: Waipoua Forest, Canterbury on a field day, the bohemian Cuba Street in Wellington, the Āpure Moana marine reserve, and the city of Rotorua. T Wei, the artist, created colorful and detailed designs for each places (for better experience). It goes with two modes: Challenge and Free Play.

Link: https://ispy.heihei.resn.co/

2) lifeat.io

lifeat.io is an all-rounder website that helps you to build a comfortable workplace. Set your own workplace with different themes. Themes: Explore, Space, Lofi, Nature, City, Beach, Window, Cafe, K-pop, Anime, Games, Library, Study w/me, Pets, Celebrity, Snow, and many more. Whether you do not have the mood to study or not, this website will sure entertain you. You can study, work, sleep, and maybe even do other crazy stuff with it. Talking about such activties, lifeat.io boosts your productivity and creativity.

Link: https://lifeat.io/

3) BrandStudio

BrandStudio is a beautiful interactive website. The webstie gives quirky animations and effects, which please the viewers to go through the website. When you to the page, you will see a question if your computer device is normal or powerful. Your choice will not affect the content that you will see. In fact, the difference whether you click normal or powerful is not striking. Now, once you have entered, you will see clocks for cities---New York, Tokyo, and Moscow. You need to select. Once you have clicked your preferred place, you can now dive in to the main course of the website (the adventure).

Link: https://bstd.ru/en/

4) Bruno Simon

Bruno Simon, creative developer living in Paris, freelancer, former lead developer at Immersive Garden, former developer at Uzik and teacher. Enter the website and you will see a 3D Car ready for driving. The website depicts a beautiful and large playfield. It enables the user to move the car left, right, up (forward), and down (backward).

Link: https://bruno-simon.com/

Stories

1) Zizo

Zizo is a stunning interactive website made by the company. The webstie uses a cel shading technique of modern games to the max. It is scroll-triggered navigation. Meaning, you will need a mouse to scroll up or down while you continue wondering to the website. As you enter, you will see a beautiful Japanese landscape. Along your journey, you will get to know more about Zizo (like how they started, value their customers, make their baranded content marketing).

Link: https://wearezizo.com/

2) makemepulse

The website was created for the year 2019. The interactive studio makemepulse is known for its highly creative solutions in the digital industry. The given website is a project made by the said studio. The website is a tribute to one of the best comic book artists of all time, Jean Giraud a.k.a. Moebius. Like Zizo, makemepulse used cel shading technique for their project, which is about the Nomadic Tribe. Nomadic Tribe is a tale about a tribe that every year (on New Year’s Eve to be precise) moves from one island to the next.

Link: https://2019.makemepulse.com/

3) Atelier Heschung

Atelier Heschung shares its story with users in an interactive way. From the beginning to making ski shoes for a team, its story depends on the user’s clever actions, as it emphasizes the importance of interactivity and makes the whole site all the more engaging.

Link: https://noel.heschung.com/en/chapitre/les-debuts-de-l-aventure/
